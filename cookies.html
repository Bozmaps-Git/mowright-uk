Cookie policy.

Every cookie MowRight UK can set, in plain English. You can switch the optional ones off any time.

What cookies are

A cookie is a small text file a website asks your browser to store. Some are essential for the site to work; others are used for analytics, advertising or personalisation. Under UK PECR and UK GDPR we have to tell you exactly what each one does and let you say no to the optional ones.

Cookies this site can set

CookieSet byPurposeDurationCategory
mw_consent MowRight UK Stores your cookie-banner choice so we don't ask again. 12 months Essential
__gads, __gpi, IDE, NID Google AdSense Ad selection, ad measurement, and frequency-capping. Only set when you accept advertising cookies. up to 13 months Advertising
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics (if enabled) Anonymous aggregate analytics — page views, traffic sources, country-level location. Only set when you accept analytics cookies. up to 24 months Analytics

Managing your choices

  • Click the "Cookie settings" link at the bottom of any page to re-open the consent banner.
  • Opt out of personalised ads across all Google services at adssettings.google.com.
  • Block or delete cookies via your browser settings. Most browsers let you allow, block, or delete cookies per site.
  • Use private/incognito mode for a session that doesn't persist any cookies after you close the window.

Disabling advertising cookies will not remove ads from the site, but the ads will be less relevant (generic rather than tailored). Disabling essential cookies will make the consent banner re-appear on every page load.

Do Not Track

Where your browser sends a "Do Not Track" or "Global Privacy Control" signal, we treat that as a refusal of optional cookies. You will still see ads from Google AdSense, but they will not be personalised.

Changes to this policy

If we add, remove or change the cookies the site uses, we will update the table above and the "last updated" date. Material changes are flagged on the homepage for at least 30 days.
