Buying Guide — Ride-On & Garden Tractor Lawnmowers

Engine hours, not years

Under 200 hours is excellent on any ride-on. Over 600 means assume the worst and price accordingly.

PTO clutch test

Engage the cutter deck. It should bite cleanly with no slip. A worn PTO clutch is a £200+ fix.

Hydrostatic transmission

Pull forward and reverse smoothly with no jolts. Belt slip under load is the most common ride-on fault.

Service history adds value

A John Deere or Honda with full dealer-stamped service adds £300–£400 to fair price. No history = price as worst-case.

Steering pull

Drive on a flat surface with hands off. Pulling left or right is a tracking or front-axle fault.
