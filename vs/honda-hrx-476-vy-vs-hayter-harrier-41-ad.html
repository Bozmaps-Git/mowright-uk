Verdict — which one to buy

Our take

The classic premium-roller decision. The Hayter is British-built and serviceable for 30 years; the Honda is heavier and wider but easier to find used. Stripers and traditionalists go Hayter; pragmatists who want one mower forever go Honda.

Pros & cons of each

Honda HRX 476 VY

Pros

  • Honda engine — bulletproof reliability

Cons
