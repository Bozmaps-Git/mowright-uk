Verdict — which one to buy

Our take

Bosch vs Gardena at premium boundary-wire. Bosch wins on the LogiCut algorithm for efficient mowing; Gardena wins on the smart-home integration ecosystem. Tech-house owners go Gardena, efficiency-obsessed owners go Bosch.

Pros & cons of each

Bosch Indego S+ 500

Pros

  • Bosch app integration with Smart Home
  • LogiCut systematic mowing pattern
  • Lighter than Husqvarna equivalents
  • Bosch parts/spares accessible

Cons

  • Boundary wire installation needed
  • 19cm cut slow on bigger lawns
  • Indego app updates have broken older units
Gardena Sileno Life 1000

Pros

  • Gardena reliability — Husqvarna Group quality at lower price
  • 1000m² coverage suits typical UK suburban lawns
  • SensorCut adapts to grass conditions
  • Quietest in class

Cons

  • Boundary wire still required
  • 22cm cut means slow on full perimeter
  • Gardena Smart app limited vs Husqvarna Connect
