Verdict — which one to buy

Our take

Premium cordless head-to-head. EGO has the better cut and longer runtime; Stihl has the rear roller (stripes) and dealer support. EGO wins for buyers without an existing Stihl tool ecosystem; Stihl wins for AP-platform owners.

Pros & cons of each

EGO LM2135E-SP

Pros

  • Genuinely petrol-replacement performance
  • EGO 56V batteries cross-compatible across full range
  • Self-propelled with variable speed
  • Quiet and clean — no fumes

Cons

  • Battery and charger eat the price
  • 5Ah battery only does ~700m² per charge
  • Replacement battery is £200+
Stihl RMA 448 TC

Pros

  • Stihl AP battery shared across pro tools
  • Rear roller — rare on cordless
  • Stihl dealer support
  • Build quality is professional grade

Cons

  • Battery and charger sold separately — adds £300+
  • Heavy at 29kg
  • Limited runtime on a single AP300 battery
