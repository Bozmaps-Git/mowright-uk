Verdict — which one to buy

Our take

Kubota sizing. The GR2120 has 20cm more cut and a bigger 21HP V-twin. Worth the upgrade only if your lawn justifies the extra deck width — for most domestic buyers the GR1600 is plenty.

Pros & cons of each

Kubota GR1600-II

Pros

  • Kubota Japanese build quality
  • WG752 V-twin reliable for 1500+ hours
  • Hydrostatic drive smooth

Cons

  • Eye-watering price

Pros

Cons

  • Heavier than the GR1600 at 380kg
  • Kubota dealer network limited
