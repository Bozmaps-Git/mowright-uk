Verdict — which one to buy

Our take

British-built rivals from the same AriensCo factory. Same engineering, slightly different badging. Westwood traditionally cheaper new; Countax has 4WD on the B65 series for serious slopes. Identical for flat lawns; Countax wins on hilly ones.
