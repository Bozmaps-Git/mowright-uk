Buying Guide — Petrol Lawnmowers

Cold-start it

A hot engine hides starting issues. Always insist on firing it from cold before you pay.

Check the oil

Pull the dipstick. Golden = recently serviced. Black or milky = avoid or knock £80 off the price.

Test self-propel under load

A flat driveway is not a test. Walk it up an incline. A slipping drive belt is the #1 fault.

Inspect the deck

Lift the mower. Look for welded repairs, cracks, or rust holes. Surface rust is fine; structural rust is not.

Honda holds value

A used Honda HRX retains 50–60% of its new price for years. Mountfield and Webb depreciate fast — that cuts both ways.
