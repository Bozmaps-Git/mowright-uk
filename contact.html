What we will reply to

  • Price corrections (with a link to the source you're seeing)
  • Models you'd like us to add (brand + model number is enough)
  • Owner tips: what broke on your mower, what you'd buy next, what a buyer should check
  • UK mower buying questions — we may publish the answer (anonymised) if it'd help others
  • Genuine press, editorial or partnership enquiries

What we won't reply to

  • Affiliate-link, sponsored-post, guest-post or "we'd love to collaborate" SEO pitches — please don't
  • Requests to write paid product reviews
  • Manufacturer offers of "free units in exchange for a positive write-up"
  • Mass cold pitches that haven't read the site
