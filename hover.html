Hover Lawnmowers

The British answer to awkward lawns. A hover mower glides on a cushion of air, which means slopes, banks, and fence edges that defeat wheeled mowers are easy. Flymo invented the category and still owns it — Hover Vac and Glider models appear on Marketplace constantly.

Buying Guide — Hover Lawnmowers

Test the lift fan

The fan housing develops cracks after years of bumping. Flip it and inspect — cracks ruin the cushion.

Cable management is the trade-off

Hover mowers need power, and the cable on a hilly lawn is genuinely fiddly. Plan your route.

Mulch vs collect

Hover Vac models collect clippings. Glider models mulch. Choose based on whether you mind not bagging.

Plastic is the build

These are not heirlooms. Sub-£40 used and the supply on Marketplace is enormous. Disposable but capable.

Awkward gardens win

If your lawn has banks, drops, or odd shapes, a hover beats every wheeled mower under £200. Specifically.
