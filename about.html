About MowRight UK

Independent UK lawnmower price guide. We compare new prices, used Facebook Marketplace and eBay averages, specs, and pros and cons across 72 popular models from 32 brands.

Why this site exists

Buying a lawnmower in the UK is harder than it should be. New prices vary wildly between B&Q, Argos, dealer websites, and direct manufacturer stores. Used prices on Facebook Marketplace and eBay swing 40% based on optimism, season, and how badly the seller wants the shed space. Most "best lawnmower" articles online are affiliate roundups that have not been near a real lawn.

MowRight UK is the AutoTrader of lawnmowers. One place to check what a mower is actually worth — new and used — before you spend your money.

Where the prices come from

  • RRP — manufacturer published list price
  • Buy Now — typical UK street price across major retailers (Amazon, B&Q, Robert Dyas, Mowers Online, manufacturer direct)
  • Used Avg — averaged from Facebook Marketplace and eBay UK sold listings, with outliers removed

Prices are reviewed periodically. They are guidance, not guarantees — local market conditions and condition affect every transaction.

What we do not do

  • Take payment from sellers, manufacturers, or retailers
  • Run affiliate links to specific listings
  • Score mowers we have not researched against rivals

We do show Google AdSense advertising. Ad placement is fixed and never influences which mowers appear or how they are ranked.
