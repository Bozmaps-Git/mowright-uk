Murray

Briggs & Stratton (KPS Capital)

Murray has been making ride-on tractors in the US since the 1920s. The red-and-yellow striped 11/30 of the 1990s is a Marketplace classic — built simple, repaired easily, parts cheap. Modern Murray tractors continue under Briggs & Stratton ownership.
