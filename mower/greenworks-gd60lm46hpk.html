Our verdict

The bottom line

The cheapest serious cordless with stripes. Performance is genuinely close to a Honda HRG. Battery resale is the weakness — if you want long-term value, EGO wins.

Pros & cons

Pros

  • 60V platform genuinely bridges petrol gap
  • Rear roller for stripes
  • Self-propelled
  • Decent runtime — 45–60 mins

Cons

  • Greenworks 60V batteries less common than EGO/Stihl
  • Heavier than EGO equivalents
  • Build quality below Husqvarna/EGO standard

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£200–340 fair used. Roller bearings, drive cable, battery health — same checks as a petrol SP. Greenworks 60V batteries depreciate fast — never pay more than £100 for a used 6Ah pack.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
