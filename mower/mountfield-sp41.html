Our verdict

The bottom line

The mower that paved the way for the SP46. Older but still capable and the used supply is huge — typically £20–40 cheaper than an SP46 in equivalent condition. If you find a clean one, snap it up.

Pros & cons

Pros

  • Self-propelled at budget price
  • Same Stiga engine family as the SP46
  • Light enough to lift into a car
  • Spares are everywhere

Cons

  • 41cm cut narrow for modern lawns
  • Plastic deck dents on stones
  • Drive cable stretches with age

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£70–140 is the realistic used range. Massive Marketplace volume — never overpay. Test self-propel under load. Drive cable replacement is £15 DIY. Avoid any with melted plastic around the muffler — sign of dry-running and overheating.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
