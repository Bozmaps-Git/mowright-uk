Expert Verdict

A premium boundary-wire robot from a brand that knows engines but is newer to robotics. The Miimo line has caught up to Husqvarna on durability — pricing is the only concern.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£900–1500 used. Honda Miimo serial-number checks confirm warranty transferability — do this on anything over £1100. Boundary wire integrity check as standard. Reset Miimo Live account before purchase.
