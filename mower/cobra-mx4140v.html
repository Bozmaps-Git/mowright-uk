Expert Verdict

A reasonable budget cordless if you do not care about ecosystem lock-in. The Greenworks G40 does the same job slightly better and has a bigger user base.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£90–170 used. Cobra batteries hold no resale value, so a tired battery destroys the deal. Always demand a 20-minute runtime demo. The deck is plastic and the wheel mounts crack — inspect them carefully.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Battery included in price
  • 40cm cut decent for the money
  • Mulch plug standard
  • Cheap entry to cordless

Cons

  • Cobra battery platform isolated — no cross-tool benefit
  • Plastic build
  • 3-year warranty but support thin
