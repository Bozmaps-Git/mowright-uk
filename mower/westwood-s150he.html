Expert Verdict

Identical engineering to the Countax B65 from the same AriensCo UK factory. Westwood badging traditionally appears at slightly lower prices. A genuine British-built tractor for serious lawns.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£1800–3500 used. Engine hours under 600 is good. PGC blower belt slipping is the most common collection fault — £40 replacement, dealer fitting recommended. Westwood/Countax service history is interchangeable; both add £300 to fair value.
