Expert Verdict

For people who care more about engine longevity than badge prestige. The Honda V-twin will run for 1500 hours with sensible service. Choose this over a JD if your priority is "still works in 15 years".

Marketplace Buying Tip

£1700–2900 used. Honda engine hours under 500 is excellent — these run forever. Test the hydrostatic forward/reverse smoothness. Look at the deck spindles — bearing seizure is the most expensive ride-on fault and these are non-trivial to replace. Honda service stamps add £400 to fair value.
