Our verdict

The bottom line

A reasonable mid-range corded electric from a brand that actually has UK dealer support. Outclassed by Bosch on motor longevity but matched on cut quality. The 5-year warranty is worth £20 of the price.

Cons

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£25–60 used. Cable damage and motor whine are the two checks. Cobra warranty is non-transferable so used buyers are on their own. Plastic deck cracks at the rear flap hinge — common fault, £15 part if you can find it.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
