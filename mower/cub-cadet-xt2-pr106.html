Expert Verdict

A solid heavy-duty alternative to JD that punches above its price. The Kohler engine is a real plus. Just be aware UK service is patchy.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£1300–2200 used. Kohler engine hours under 400 is good. Cub Cadet steering box wears — check for play. PTO clutch must engage cleanly without slip. Service history is harder to verify (smaller dealer network) so weight that into the price.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Kohler engine — premium American powerplant
  • Wide deck cuts fast
  • Hydrostatic with cruise control
  • Heavy-duty construction

Cons

  • Cub Cadet UK dealer support thin
  • Imported parts can be slow
  • Steering is heavier than JD
