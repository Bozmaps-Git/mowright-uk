Expert Verdict

The right tool for a postage-stamp lawn. Do not buy if your lawn is bigger than 100m². Bosch makes them durable — a fair urban mower.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£50–110 used. PowerForAll battery is £40+ secondhand, so a battery-included sale is the only one worth doing. Check the battery cells via runtime — a healthy 4Ah does 25 mins; under 12 mins is a dead pack. Bosch motor is brushless and rarely fails.
