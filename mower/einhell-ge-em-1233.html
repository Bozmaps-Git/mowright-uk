Expert Verdict

A good first mower for a tiny terraced garden. If your lawn is over 40m², look elsewhere. Disposable but fine for the price.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£20–45 used. Honestly, just buy new for £89 unless you find one for £30. Check the cable and motor noise. No serviceable parts — it either runs or it does not.
