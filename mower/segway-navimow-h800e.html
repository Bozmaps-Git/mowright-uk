Expert Verdict

Segway took longer than Husqvarna to get the basics right but the H800E is solid. Reliable and well-supported now. The 18cm cut is the only limitation — it is slow.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£1000–1700 used. VisionFence base must be included with calibration intact. Test it in mixed lighting if possible — heavy rain can fool the cameras. Segway accounts transfer cleanly if seller does the factory reset; insist on this.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • VisionFence camera + RTK is reliable
  • Segway support is improving in UK
  • No boundary wire
  • Quiet and tidy

Cons

  • 18cm cut is small for an 800m² lawn
  • VisionFence struggles in rain
