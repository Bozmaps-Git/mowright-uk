Our verdict

The bottom line

For people who liked the IZY 466 but want the cut width of an HRX without the price. Same simple format, just bigger. If your lawn is between 1000–1500m² and stripes don't matter, this is the smarter Honda buy than stretching to a 537.

Pros & cons

Pros

  • Honda GCV170 — bigger engine for the wider deck
  • 53cm cut beats the 466 by a useful margin
  • Same IZY simplicity (rope start, single drive lever)
  • Proper Honda dealer support

Cons

  • Heavier than the 466
  • Still no rear roller (no stripes)
  • Wider deck means gate clearance issues for some

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£420–540 used is fair. Drive belt under load is the wear point — slipping when self-propel is engaged means a £30 replacement. Check the wider deck underside for impact damage; the 53cm cut is more likely to clip stones in narrow lawns.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
