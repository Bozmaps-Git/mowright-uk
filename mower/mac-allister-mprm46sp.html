Expert Verdict

The default "I just need a petrol mower from B&Q" buy. Performs above its price thanks to the Briggs engine, but build quality is honestly below Mountfield. Decent value new, fair used.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£70–160 used. Briggs engine is the only durable bit — if it runs cleanly cold, the rest can be fixed. Drive cable and plastic chassis cracks are the common faults. Never pay more than £150 — there is always another at B&Q for £270 new with warranty.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • B&S engine — well known and serviceable
  • Self-propelled at budget price
  • 46cm cut suits average UK lawn

Cons

  • Plastic everything — flimsier than Mountfield
  • 3-year B&Q warranty is generous but limited to original buyer
