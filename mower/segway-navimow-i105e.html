Our verdict

The bottom line

A reasonable wire-free robot for compact gardens. Bigger than the i108E with similar features. Worth checking against the Mammotion YUKA Mini at similar money — Mammotion is slightly newer and the LUBA family has better long-term reputation.

Pros & cons

Pros

  • RTK no-wire setup at compact-garden price
  • Segway Smart app maturing
  • Compact and quiet
  • 3-year Segway warranty

Cons

  • 18cm cut very slow
  • RTK signal sensitive to obstructions
  • Segway brand still building UK presence

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£500–900 used. RTK base must be included with calibration intact. Most i105Es are recent — confirm purchase date and warranty status. Reset Segway account before purchase. Avoid units stored outside; dock electronics corrode in damp UK weather.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
