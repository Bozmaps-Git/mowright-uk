Our verdict

The bottom line

The accessible robot. Half the price of a Husqvarna 305 used, smaller coverage, simpler. Will not last 15 years like a Husqvarna but costs the right amount. Reasonable starter robot for a small flat lawn.

Pros & cons

Pros

  • Flymo brand recognition

  • Quiet and tidy

Cons

  • 250m² coverage tiny
  • 17cm cut means slow on full perimeter
  • Battery cells degrade quickly

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£100–200 used. Boundary wire integrity is everything — walk every metre. Battery cells are the second fail point; demand a runtime test. Reset the Flymo app account before purchase. Avoid units stored outdoors year-round (dock contacts corrode).

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
