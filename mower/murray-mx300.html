Our verdict

The bottom line

The current entry Murray. Same cut width as the heritage 11/30 but with collection, mulching, and modern controls. Good value new but used supply is small in the UK — Mountfield 1430M and 1530H are the more obvious rivals at this price.

Pros & cons

Pros

  • Cheapest current Murray ride-on
  • Briggs 7150 — well-supported engine
  • Mulch and collect
  • 30in deck cuts efficiently

Cons

  • Murray UK distribution thin since the B&S restructure
  • Steering linkage feels light
  • Plastic bonnet

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£600–1000 used. Briggs 7150 hours under 200 is excellent. Confirm Murray UK warranty status — distribution gaps mean some units are direct-import. Murray dealer support is via Briggs & Stratton service network.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
