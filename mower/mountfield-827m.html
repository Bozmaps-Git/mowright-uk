Our verdict

The bottom line

The right Mountfield ride-on for buyers who want the convenience of a tractor without spending Mountfield 1530H money. Manual transmission is the trade-off — but for under £1500 new it is the cheapest way into a credible UK ride-on.

Pros & cons

Pros

  • Cheapest manual-transmission Mountfield ride-on
  • 66cm deck fits narrow gates
  • Stiga engine — universal parts
  • 170L collection bag

Cons

  • Manual gearbox — less smooth than hydrostatic
  • Single-cylinder engine works hard
  • Smaller deck means slow on big lawns

Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£550–950 used. Engine hours under 250 is good. Manual transmission needs a smooth shift through gears; sticking is usually linkage adjustment (£30 dealer fix). Steering box wears — check for play before paying.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
