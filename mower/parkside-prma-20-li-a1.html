Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£25–60 used. Insist the 20V battery is included — without it, this mower is useless. Battery cells degrade like any other; demand a runtime test (15 minutes minimum on a healthy 4Ah). Parkside warranty is non-transferable so used buyers are on their own.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
