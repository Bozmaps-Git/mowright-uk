Expert Verdict

The Lidl Middle Aisle special that has built a genuine cult following. If you already own Parkside 20V tools, this slots into the ecosystem at a price that no other brand can match. Surprisingly capable for the money.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£25–60 used. Insist the 20V battery is included — without it, this mower is useless. Battery cells degrade like any other; demand a runtime test (15 minutes minimum on a healthy 4Ah). Parkside warranty is non-transferable so used buyers are on their own.
