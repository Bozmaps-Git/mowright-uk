Expert Verdict

The robot lawnmower that proved the category. Handles UK weather, easy parts supply, dealer-serviceable. The 305 is the entry — fine for under 600m² lawns with simple shapes.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£300–550 used. Boundary wire integrity is everything — walk the perimeter and check for breaks (visible cuts in the wire). Confirm app account is transferable or factory-reset performed before purchase. Charging contacts on the dock corrode — clean them with a wire brush before judging battery life.
