Buying second-hand

Used-market tip

£300–550 used. Boundary wire integrity is everything — walk the perimeter and check for breaks (visible cuts in the wire). Confirm app account is transferable or factory-reset performed before purchase. Charging contacts on the dock corrode — clean them with a wire brush before judging battery life.

Where to look: Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree are usually 20–30% cheaper than eBay UK for petrol mowers because most sellers want local pickup. eBay tends to win on cordless and electric (lighter, easier to ship). Always insist on a starting demonstration before paying.
