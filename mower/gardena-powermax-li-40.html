Expert Verdict

A neat continental-style cordless that suits small UK lawns. Lacks mulch and roller so functionality is basic. Gardena hold value better than Worx, less than EGO.

Marketplace Buying Tip

£100–180 used. Confirm PowerForAll 40V battery is included. The handle latches can break — check they lock cleanly. No mulch plug is a real limitation; budget for bagging every cut.
